Project Overview

The ENSAN Charity Head Office is a multi-storey administrative facility located in Riyadh. The project required precise architectural BIM development, coordination with structural and MEP systems, and compliance with client-specific BIM standards.

Role & Responsibilities

Architectural Model Development

Developed detailed architectural BIM models from schematic design to construction documentation.

Multidisciplinary Coordination

Coordinated architectural elements with structural and MEP disciplines to avoid conflicts.

Quality Control

Performed model audits and ensured compliance with project naming conventions and LOD standards.

Drawing Extraction

Generated coordinated shop drawings and construction documentation directly from BIM models.

Tools & Platforms

Autodesk Revit, Navisworks Manage, BIM 360, ISO 19650 standards.

Project Impact

Improved documentation accuracy and minimized coordination issues through structured BIM workflows, enabling efficient design progression and high-quality construction deliverables.
