Armed Forces Hospital – Abu Arish

Architecture BIM Specialist | East Consulting Engineering Center (ECEC)

Project Overview

The Armed Forces Hospital in Abu Arish is a large-scale healthcare facility requiring high-level coordination between architectural, structural, and MEP disciplines. The project involved complex hospital planning standards, strict compliance requirements, and precision-based BIM workflows.

Role & Responsibilities

Architectural BIM Development

Developed and maintained LOD 300–400 architectural models aligned with healthcare standards.

Interdisciplinary Coordination

Coordinated with structural and MEP teams to ensure accurate spatial planning and service integration.

Clash Detection & Resolution

Performed clash analysis using Navisworks and facilitated coordination meetings.

Compliance & Documentation

Ensured adherence to client BIM standards, naming conventions, and model quality checks.

Tools & Platforms

Autodesk Revit, Navisworks Manage, BIM 360, ISO 19650 workflows, healthcare design standards.

Project Impact

Strengthened multidisciplinary coordination and minimized design conflicts through structured BIM reviews, improving model accuracy and enhancing overall project efficiency during documentation and design development phases.
