SABIC Jubail Main Building – Al Jubail

BIM Modeler | Shapoorji Pallonji Mid East LLC

Project Overview

The SABIC Jubail Main Building is a major corporate and industrial headquarters facility serving one of Saudi Arabia’s largest petrochemical organizations. The project required precise BIM modeling, coordination, and documentation to support construction delivery.

Role & Responsibilities

Architectural BIM Modeling

Developed detailed architectural models up to LOD 400 for construction documentation.

Interdisciplinary Coordination

Collaborated with structural and MEP teams to ensure clash-free model integration.

Drawing Extraction

Generated coordinated construction drawings and schedules directly from BIM models.

Quality Control

Maintained model accuracy and compliance with project BIM standards and client requirements.

Tools & Platforms

Autodesk Revit, Navisworks Manage, AutoCAD, BIM 360 workflows.

Project Impact

Delivered high-quality coordinated BIM models that supported efficient construction documentation and minimized design conflicts, contributing to streamlined project execution.
